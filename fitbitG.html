FITBIT @ IDEO

IDEOers are earning points by taking steps and making connections with other Fitbit/ers:
+ 1 point for every step they take
+ 1 point for every 5 steps taken by connections within their Collective
+ 1 point for every 10 steps taken by connections outside their Collective.

You’ll know a connection has been made every time you see a pair of Fitbiters getting their picture taken
in front of the Connection Graph poster in San Francisco or Palo Alto.
Once each pair posts their pics to the Fitbit Story Stream (an internal Facebook Group)
they’ll be earning points from each others’ steps.
